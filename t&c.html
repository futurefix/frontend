2. Withdrawal & Unlocking

Withdrawals are disabled for the entire plan period. On plan maturity:

  • Funds (principal + any earned profit) will be unlocked automatically on the maturity date.
  • Once unlocked, you may request a withdrawal using the standard withdrawal process in your account.
  • Automatic unlocking does not require additional action from the investor; it occurs at 00:00 UTC on the maturity date.

3. Early Exit

Early or partial withdrawals are not permitted. This includes:

  • Partial redemption of principal before maturity.
  • Closing the plan and withdrawing before the end date.

In exceptional cases (e.g., demonstrated financial hardship, legal requirement), the company may consider early exits on a case-by-case basis. Any approved early exit may carry penalties, reduced profit, or both.

4. Profit Access & Distribution

Profits or returns generated by the plan are the property of the investor but remain locked until maturity. Details:

  • Profit calculations and the final credited amount will be shown in your account when the plan unlocks.
  • If a plan specifies periodic profit credits (e.g., monthly payouts), those credits will be visible in your account but will remain non-withdrawable until the plan matures unless otherwise stated.

5. Start Date, Maturity Date & Notifications

The plan start date is the date of successful payment confirmation. The maturity date is computed by adding the plan duration to the start date.

  • We will notify you by email 7 days and 1 day before maturity, and again when the plan unlocks.
  • It is the investor's responsibility to ensure contact information is up-to-date.
