Privacy Policy — Account Information

This policy explains how FutureFix accounts collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information — including your account's Secret ID used for login.

1. What we collect

When you create an account we may collect:

  • Full name, email address, phone number.
  • Account credentials: a Secret ID (unique identifier) and a password. The Secret ID is used for login and account recovery workflows where applicable.
  • Optional profile information you choose to add.

2. How we use your Secret ID

  • The Secret ID is a unique identifier that helps authenticate and identify your account during login and verification flows.
  • We treat the Secret ID as personal and confidential information. It is kept private and is never displayed publicly.
  • Wherever possible we store only a secure, hashed form of sensitive login identifiers so that plain values are not readable in our systems.

3. How we protect your Secret ID and other personal data

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is protected using industry-standard TLS/HTTPS.
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive account identifiers and credentials are stored using encryption and/or secure hashing algorithms so raw values are not stored in plain text.
  • Access controls: Only authorized employees with a legitimate business need (for example, site reliability or support engineers) can access account data, and even then access is limited and logged.
  • Internal safeguards: We use role-based access, two-factor authentication for administrative access, and regular audits to reduce risk of unauthorized access.
  • Data minimization: We keep only the information necessary to provide the service and delete or anonymize data when it is no longer required.
Note: While we take strong technical and organizational measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. If a security incident occurs, we will follow applicable law, notify affected users promptly, and take steps to mitigate harm.

4. Company staff & third-party access

  • Company staff do not have unrestricted access to your Secret ID in plain form. When possible we store only hashed or tokenized representations.
  • We may share account data with trusted third-party service providers (for example: payment processors, email providers, analytics) only when necessary to provide the service. These providers are contractually required to keep data secure and only use it for agreed purposes.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Your choices & rights

  • Access: You can view and update most of your account information in your account settings.
  • Deletion: You may request account deletion. After deletion we will remove or anonymize personal data subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Password & Secret ID safety: Do not share your Secret ID or password with others. If you suspect your account is compromised, change your password immediately and contact support.

6. Retention

We retain your account data for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When data is no longer needed it will be deleted or anonymized.

7. Children's privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us to request deletion.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy with a revised "Last updated" date and, where required by law, notify you of the changes.
